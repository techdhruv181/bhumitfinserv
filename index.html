Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to start investing in Mutual Funds?

The best way to start is through a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). It allows you to invest small amounts regularly, building wealth over time while benefiting from rupee cost averaging.

How much life insurance cover do I need?

A general rule of thumb is to have a term insurance cover that is 10 to 15 times your annual income, plus any outstanding debts or future major expenses like children's education.

What documents are needed for ITR filing?

Typically, you need your PAN card, Aadhaar card, Form 16 (for salaried), bank statements, and investment proofs (for deductions under 80C, 80D, etc.).
